Kluge Process: Name__________________________________________ Date________________
Verify First Article meets all requirements IR____________________________________Date_______________
  1. Prepare appropriate Foil, Emboss, Die Cutting, Perforating, or Scoring Die.
  2. Foil part #’s must match discrete job; check Lot# of each item against pick list; lot must be clearly assigned on pick list.
